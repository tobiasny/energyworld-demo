# NCS Overview — Customer Interview

## 1. Company & Branding

- **Project name**: NCS Overview
- **Branding**: Default design system (no custom branding)

## 2. Purpose

- **What it does**: Gives an overview of oil and gas production of all fields on the Norwegian Continental Shelf
- **Primary user**: Analysts

## 3. Core Features

1. View a map of the fields on the continental shelf (most important)
2. Update production values per field per year
3. See historic development of production per field

## 4. Data

- **Field**: name, location (lat/lng), status (producing/shut down/under development)
- **Production record**: field, year, oil volume, gas volume

## 5. Pages & Navigation

1. **Map view** (main page) — map with field markers, clicking a field opens its details
2. **Field detail view** — historical production chart + editable production table

## 6. Scope & Priorities

- **MVP**: Map with clickable fields + production chart
- **Fast follow**: Editable production data per field per year
- **Out of scope**: Nothing explicitly excluded
